Who am I?

I am Jonathan Boulton and I like to create. My interest in software development started in 2017, my first year of college, when I saw an a friend coding for a project in his CS class. I knew imedeiatly it was incredibly complex and I would never grasp a single concept. He then started to show me how to download a virtual machine and install my first linux distro. I learned basic file traversal through the terminal and usage of some tools. I was a well boneified script kiddie. Over time I became totally mesmorized by my career, at the time, as a chef and put the idea of any interaction with technology purely surface level. I was exclusively an end user.

I moved to NYC to work in michilen star fine dining. Turns out spending 12-13 hours a day sweating over a stove is actually really intensive work. After much thought I decided to put my head back in the ring and fully focus on technology again.

The transition was surprising. All the work is laid out ready for an aspiring software enthusist to take hold. I started with learning the basics of Python and creating some simple projects. Then moved to HTML, CSS, and now javascript.
